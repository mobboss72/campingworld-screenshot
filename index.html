Welcome to the Compliance Screenshot Tool

This tool is designed to capture compliance screenshots from Camping World RV listings, specifically targeting hover states for price and payment details. It ensures legal timestamping for audit purposes.

How to Use:

  1. Enter a valid Camping World stock number (e.g., 2319928) in the field below.
  2. Click 'Run Capture' to generate screenshots.
  3. View the resulting images with embedded timestamps and SHA-256 hashes, displayed side by side.
