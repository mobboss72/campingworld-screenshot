CW Compliance Screenshot Tool

Welcome

This tool captures compliance screenshots from Camping World RV listings, including hover states for price and payment details. Each capture includes a legal timestamp and SHA-256 hash for verification.

How to Use:

  1. Enter a valid Camping World stock number (e.g., 2319928).
  2. Click Run Capture to generate screenshots.
  3. View the resulting images with timestamps and hashes.
