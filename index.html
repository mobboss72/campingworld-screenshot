Key Features

Built for accuracy, speed, and indisputable proof of disclosure.

Tamper-Proof Audit

Every capture includes an RFC 3161 timestamp and a SHA-256 hash, creating an indisputable record of when the page content was seen.

Automated Screenshots

The capture is run from an Oregon-based server, simulating a local user experience to accurately reflect state-specific pricing and payment information.
