How It Works

1

Location Simulation

Select one of 5 Oregon CW locations. The system spoofs browser geolocation with precise GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) and injects the corresponding ZIP code. This ensures the CW website detects the correct location and displays accurate regional pricing, financing terms, and availability.

2

Automated Screenshot Capture

A headless Chromium browser navigates to the listing and automatically triggers pricing tooltips. Full-page screenshots capture both the total price breakdown (MSRP, discounts, fees) and estimated payment details including APR, loan term, down payment, and monthly payment amount.

3

Cryptographic Verification

Each screenshot is processed with SHA-256 cryptographic hashing to create a unique fingerprint. Combined with UTC capture timestamps and HTTPS server dates, this provides tamper-evident proof of authenticity for legal documentation.

4

Professional PDF Generation

The system compiles both screenshots in a comprehensive PDF report including all metadata, SHA-256 hashes, capture timestamps, and verification details. The PDF automatically downloads to your computer as the permanent legal record.

5

Database Audit Trail

All capture metadata is stored in a secure database for future reference. While screenshots are automatically cleaned up after 90 days to prevent storage bloat, the audit trail with hashes and timestamps remains permanently accessible.
